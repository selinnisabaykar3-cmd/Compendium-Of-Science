What is Compendium?

Compendium of Science is an AI-powered research platform designed to help high school and university students discover, understand and analyze scientific knowledge more efficiently.

Why Compendium?

To empower students, researchers, and innovators by making scientific knowledge accessible, understandable, and actionable. Through AI-powered tools and curated academic resources, Compendium of Science helps transform curiosity into discovery and ideas into innovation.

🔬 Research Library

Explore scientific topics, articles and learning resources.

🤖 AI Assistant

Ask scientific questions and receive explanations.

📚 Learning Resources

Educational materials and study guides coming soon.

Our Mission

To empower students, researchers, and innovators by making scientific knowledge accessible, understandable and actionable.

Story of Compendium

As a student conducting research projects, I realized that finding relevant scientific papers and understanding them was difficult and time-consuming. I created Compendium of Science to help students navigate scientific literature more efficiently through AI-powered tools.
